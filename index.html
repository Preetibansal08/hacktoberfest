Hacktoberfest 2024

Join the Global Celebration of Open Source!

Hacktoberfest is an annual event held throughout the month of October, where developers from around the world come together to contribute to open-source projects on GitHub. Organized by DigitalOcean, Hacktoberfest is a month-long celebration of open-source software and is open to everyone, from seasoned developers to beginners.

How to Participate

To participate, simply sign up on the official Hacktoberfest website and make four or more pull requests to open-source projects on GitHub during the month of October. Whether it’s fixing bugs, writing documentation, or adding new features, every contribution counts!

Why Contribute?

Hacktoberfest is a fantastic way to get involved in the open-source community, improve your coding skills, and connect with other developers. Plus, if you successfully complete the challenge, you’ll earn a limited-edition Hacktoberfest t-shirt or plant a tree as a reward for your efforts.
